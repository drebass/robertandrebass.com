The Fear of Starting

by Andre Bass

You can call it whatever you want. Blank canvas syndrome, listening to your empty brain, lack of confidence, self consciousness. You’d be right on all accounts. I’ve had a number of starts which ultimately haven’t gone anywhere. For a long time I’ve been able to excuse it with flimsy reasons and a backpack of decisions that I thought I had to make before I could really start.

I had so many questions. What blog platform do I use? What should it look like? What do I write about? Who cares what I say? You get the point. For a college student that is a lot of work. WHile the web was great in answering some of these questions, I ultimately knew no one who had one, so I took the risk and started one on my own.

I’m still thinking alot about all of these issues and looking to the web for inspiration. So here's to anyone who is unsure if they want to start a blog. Go for it. Take the chance. Step over the void of questions. Make a account on WordPress, find a website template you like, host your site with BlueHost and find your niche. It’s out there, I promise you that.
