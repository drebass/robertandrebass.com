Nota was mobile development for a notetaking app, an alternative to traditional notetaking methods.

Nota was a mobile environment where users could upload, save and search their notes in a fast and convenient way. A 3 month delivery timeline for this project required some quick decision making, the key consideration being which tools will save us the most time! We chose Swift as the foundation to take advantage of it’s simple setup, painless data binding and simple flow features. The app went live at the end of timeline and was then pulled.
