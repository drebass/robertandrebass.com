About Me

I'm a third year student at Colorado State University expecting to graduate in May 2019. I especially enjoy being a front-end engineer or simply being creative, I enjoy bridging the gap between engineering and design — combining my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create a beautiful product. My goal is to always build applications that are scalable and efficient under the hood while providing engaging, pixel-perfect user experiences. When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably skiing, cruising around on my skateboard, or crossing off another item on my bucket list.
